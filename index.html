Building Zimbabwe’s Future, One Project at a Time.

Fossil Contracting is a wholly Zimbabwean owned civil and building construction company focused on infrastructure development, earthworks, mining services, and energy projects.

Established in 2010, Fossil Contracting has rapidly grown into one of Zimbabwe’s leading construction companies, delivering complex and transformative projects across sectors. Our strength lies in our experienced workforce, modern equipment fleet, and unwavering commitment to quality and safety. Whether it’s roads, dams, housing, or renewable energy, we are helping shape a resilient and prosperous Zimbabwe.

MR. O. CHIMUKA

(MSU): Bcoms Economics (UNISA): Hons HPA (UZ): Dip ED (UZ)

Obey is the founding Managing Director of Fossil. He has over 18 years of experience in mining, construction and management consultancy. He was groomed through Old Mutual and ILO as a Financial Consultant and Economic and Management Consultant respectively. He has also sat on the boards of ZMDC, Marange Resources, Amari Platinum, Sakunda Supplies, Sabi Gold Mine, as well as SIDA-Gender Fund and currently, he sits on the board of Great Dyke investments. Obey Chimuka is also the chairman of Freda Holdings, which owns Freda Rebecca Gold Mine Shamva Gold Mine & Jena Gold Mine, which are the largest gold producing mines in Zimbabwe. He also sits as a Non-Executive Director of BNC, which is Zimbabwe's exclusively, listed Nickel mine. He also sits on the board of GDI, which is Zimbabwe's new, & starting to operate PGM Metals (Platinum Group of Metals Mine). He's also a board member for Zim Alloys which is Zimbabwe second largest Chrome Mining Company both by deposit & by mining volume. He's widely commented business personal in the industry and sits on various other charity boards and institutions.

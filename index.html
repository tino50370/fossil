Building Zimbabwe’s Future, One Project at a Time.

Fossil Contracting is a wholly Zimbabwean owned civil and building construction company focused on infrastructure development, earthworks, mining services, and energy projects.

Established in 2010, Fossil Contracting has rapidly grown into one of Zimbabwe’s leading construction companies, delivering complex and transformative projects across sectors. Our strength lies in our experienced workforce, modern equipment fleet, and unwavering commitment to quality and safety. Whether it’s roads, dams, housing, or renewable energy, we are helping shape a resilient and prosperous Zimbabwe.

Obey Chimuka
Group CEO
Our mission is to provide world-class infrastructure solutions through innovation, strategic partnerships, and sustainable practices. We strive to exceed client expectations, enhance national development, and empower local communities through job creation and skills development.
Our vision is to be the leading construction and infrastructure development partner in Zimbabwe and beyond, recognized for excellence, integrity, and our lasting impact on society and the environment.

Your Trusted Construction Partner in Zimbabwe

Fossil Contracting undertakes a wide range of projects that include Agriculture, Mining, Residential and Industrial infrastructure. Our scope of work has also included the construction of earth dams and canals in support of Zimbabwe's key economic predicate, Agriculture. In the Mining industry, we have constructed leach tank foundations; support structures for mine shafts and conveyor belt systems.

We have made our name in the provision of high-quality residential infrastructure. Using our depth of experience, we are able to provide full turnkey solutions to meet and exceed customer expectations. For industry, we have done a lot of work in the Petrol Chemical industry for multinational companies as well as local blue-chip companies. Details of the projects are highlighted in the project section.

The company has become a leading brand in land development through turnkey solutions based on PPPs. The solutions are delivered through partnership with other consultants, contractors, local authorities and ﬁnancing partners. The land development model entails identifying urban land, entering into PPPs with local authorities, working with reputable consultants to do all designs, layout drawings, engineering drawings, topographic surveys, and actual construction of water and sewer main lines, as well as reticulation and construction of roads.

The company has carried out vast concrete works projects using reinforced concrete and various technologies to achieve diﬀerent desired concrete properties. We have mastered the art of designing specialist concrete and have skills to make virtually any type of concrete as may be required by our clients. We have onsite batching plant equipment, and we also utilize onsite premix concrete.

Our state-of-the-art Asphalt Plants oﬀer solutions that cater for all market requirements. We own two Asphalt Plants, a stationary one and a mobile one. Both allow us to oﬀer integrated services to our business operations and to the market at large. Both have a combined output of 300 tones per hour.

Our capabilities in this sector includes construction of diﬀerent types of roads such as ﬂexible pavements which are surfaced with Bitumen, either spray and chip or, hot or cold asphalt, rigid concrete pavements of diﬀerent designs and gravel roads. Our work also includes road rehabilitation, drainage works, culverts, open drains, gabion walls and concrete bridges. Our most recent works include reconstruction of the 13km Selous-Ngezi road in partnership with Zimplats which was done in a record of 3 months, rehabilitation of roads for City of Harare and Asphalt surfacing.

Our bulk earth works involves bulk stock piling, excavations, load and hauling operations across Zimbabwe. We have got a medium ﬂeet of dozers, excavators and tippers that have been used in gravel stock piling, open cast chrome and coal mining. Our projects include ZIMSCO chrome mining in Shurugwi, Gravel Stock piling of Group 5 for the rehabilitation of the Plumtree – Mutare road and also on the airport road. We are also doing bulk earth moving for desilting of several farm dams.

As demand for our services has increased with clients asking us to take on greater roles in their projects, we set up a team of well experienced craftsmen and artisans in our building works section. To date we have undertaken complex residential projects for clients who have world class requirement and speciﬁcations.

We hire our plant to the Private Sector and also the Public Sector such as the Department of Roads for works including bulk earth moving, dam construction & road construction and to the mining sector for works such as gravel stock piling, outcast chrome and coal mining.

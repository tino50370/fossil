Who We Are

Building a Legacy of Excellence
in Civil Engineering

Fossil Contracting is a Zimbabwean Contracting ﬁrm whose bouquet of service oﬀerings comprises Civil Works, Contract Mining, Road Construction, Earth Works, Building & Structural Works, Asphalt Production and Plant Hire. Since its oﬃcial inception in 2010 (though it traces its maiden operations back to 2000), the company has grown in leaps and bounds. The company is driven by a ﬂeet footed and experienced management team, with support from a staﬀ compliment of over 100 employees, whose spectrum of specialization runs across operators, technicians, engineers, tradesmen and contract employees.

Fossil is a Category A ranked member of the ZBCA, Category A ranked member of CIFOZ & Category A ranked member of the Ministry of Local Government, Public Works & National Housing, which accolades are the highest possible rankings for any construction company in Zimbabwe. Fossil's portfolio thus attests to the number of high value and well executed projects. The company's reputation has seen it securing contracts with high proﬁle organizations such as Zimplats, Nyaradzo, Econet, Zinara, Unki, Group, Government and local Authorities. Fossil Contracting is expanding its footprint into the region and has established operations in South Africa, Zambia and is look forward to enter the Mozambiquean Market in pursuit of delivering a top class customer experience. Currently working towards attaining ISO 9001:2015 certiﬁcation for quality management systems by mid-2020, Fossil Contracting owns an asset base that includes more than 100 major items of earthmoving, road construction, surfacing and concrete equipment, as well as a large quantity of ancillary equipment.

Fossil is committed to ensuring zero harm at all its sites and in its operations. To that end Fossil has a vibrant S.H.E.Q department that ensures uncompromising adherence to standard quality checks, whilst being cognizant of requisite health and safety procedures. This helps in avoiding injuries as the Fossil team conducts thorough Hazard Identiﬁcation and Risk Assessment procedures before commencing work duties each day.

WE KEEP GETTING BETTER & BETTER

Our ISO journey:
Quality is a top priority in our business

As a civil engineering ﬁrm, we are involved in civil engineering projects focused on developing infrastructure in our country. These structures are meant to last through ages. With this background, quality has been a top priority in our business operations. In October 2018, we decided to have a registered quality management system. That is when our journey began, spearheaded by the director-BMS Mrs Chimuka.

  • October 2018
    Initiating the Journey

    Fossil Contracting launched its ISO journey with ISO 9001:2015 awareness training conducted by the Standards Association of Zimbabwe (SAZ), attended by directors and staff across all departments.

  • November 2018
    Training on Development & Implementation

    The team underwent ISO 9001:2015 Development and Implementation training, empowering them to create customized, in-house procedures for quality management.

  • February 2019
    Documentation Training

    A documentation team attended training on Quality Management System (QMS) documentation at SAZ to align Fossil’s procedures with ISO 9001:2015 standards.

  • March–August 2019
    Gap Analysis & Drafting Procedures

    Fossil conducted a gap analysis and completed the draft documentation of its QMS by August 31, 2019.

  • September–December 2019
    Awareness Campaign

    Company-wide awareness training on QMS procedures was rolled out to all sites, with a focus on change management and adoption.

  • January 2020
    Internal Audit Planning

    An audit plan was developed in preparation for Fossil’s internal ISO 9001:2015 compliance audits.

  • April–December 2020
    Pandemic Disruption

    The COVID-19 lockdowns delayed Fossil’s internal audits; virtual work models were adopted to maintain progress.

  • March 2021
    First Internal Audit

    The company conducted its first internal ISO audit, followed by a root cause analysis and implementation of corrective actions.

  • May–June 2021
    SAZ Certification Audits

    Fossil submitted its QMS documentation to SAZ and successfully passed both the initial and follow-up audits.

  • August 2021
    ISO 9001:2015 Certification Achieved

    Fossil Contracting officially received ISO 9001:2015 certification from the Standards Association of Zimbabwe, marking a major milestone in its quality management journey.
